The Pittsburgh Steelers are a professional National Football League team. They are in the National Football Conference, North Division, along with the Cinncinati Bengals, Baltimore Ravens, and Cleveland Browns. They have won six Super Bowls, IX, X, XIII, XIV, XL, and XLIII. They are the only team to have won six super bowls. They have made the playoffs 31 times, won their division 23 times, and won their conference eight times. They were founded in 1933 and owned at that time by Art Rooney. They joined the NFL as the Pittsburgh Pirates, but changed to the Steelers prior to the 1940 season.
